About The Event

Western Canada's Premier Elite Showcase

The Rodeo Showcase is a high-performance hockey showcase designed to give elite young players across Western Canada the opportunity to compete at the highest level — and be seen by the people who matter.

Held in Calgary, Alberta, this invite-only event brings together the top Tier 1 and above programs in the U15 and U18 categories for a weekend of elite-level competition, skill evaluation, and exposure.

Whether you're a player looking to take your game to the next level, or a program seeking top-tier competition, The Rodeo Showcase is where it happens.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Rodeo Showcase is an invite-only, elite showcase open to U15 and U18 hockey teams with a Tier 1 classification or above. Teams must be registered with an official minor hockey association in Canada. If you're unsure whether your team qualifies, reach out and we'll assess eligibility.

The Rodeo Showcase takes place in Calgary, Alberta in the spring of 2027. Specific dates and arena locations will be announced in the coming months. Sign up for updates via the registration form above to be notified as soon as dates are confirmed.

All registered teams are guaranteed a minimum of 3 games during the round-robin phase. Teams that advance to the semi-finals or championship round will play additional games. We design the schedule to maximize ice time and competitive play for every team.

We have confirmed and expected attendance from WHL, AJHL, and major junior scouts, as well as NCAA and U SPORTS collegiate program representatives. A full list of confirmed scouts will be published closer to the event date. We take scout attendance seriously — this showcase is built around giving players real exposure.

Registration pricing will be announced in 2026 as we finalize event details. We are committed to keeping fees competitive with other elite-level showcases. Early registrants may be eligible for discounted rates. Submit your interest via the registration form and you'll be first to know when pricing is released.
