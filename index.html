About The Event

Western Canada's Premier Elite Showcase

The Rodeo Showcase is a high-performance hockey showcase designed to give elite young players across Western Canada the opportunity to compete at the highest level — and be seen by the people who matter.

Players register individually and are placed onto teams for the weekend. Want to play alongside a friend? Simply request them during registration and we'll do our best to make it happen.

Whether you're chasing a junior opportunity or a collegiate path, The Rodeo Showcase is where it happens.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Rodeo Showcase is open to individual players in the U15 and U18 age divisions who play at a Tier 1 level or above. Players must be registered with an official minor hockey association in Canada. You don't need a team — just register yourself and we handle the rest.

The Rodeo Showcase takes place in Calgary, Alberta in the spring of 2027. Specific dates and arena locations will be announced in the coming months. Sign up for updates via the registration form above to be notified as soon as dates are confirmed.

All players are guaranteed a minimum of 4 games during the round-robin and playoff phase. We design the schedule to maximize ice time and exposure for every player.

We have confirmed and expected attendance from WHL, AJHL, and major junior scouts, as well as NCAA and U SPORTS collegiate program representatives. A full list of confirmed scouts will be published closer to the event date. We take scout attendance seriously — this showcase is built around giving players real exposure.

Individual player registration pricing will be announced in 2026 as we finalize event details. We are committed to keeping fees accessible for elite-level players. Early registrants may be eligible for discounted rates. Submit your interest and you'll be first to know when pricing is released.
